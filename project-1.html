Project Overview

As a freelance project, I was tasked with designing and developing a website for a mosaic art business. The project required me to use my skills in web development and design to create a visually appealing site that showcased the business's unique and intricate mosaic pieces. To complete the project within the short timeline, I started with researching the business, its competitors, and the target audience to identify the best design and functionality approach for the site. Then, I designed the site's layout, navigation, and functionality, ensuring it was user-friendly and easy to navigate.

After the design was finalized, I developed the site, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development tools to create a responsive, fast-loading, and fully functional website. During the project, I made sure to communicate regularly with the client, providing regular updates and seeking feedback to ensure the final product met their expectations.
